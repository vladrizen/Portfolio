Company Agent (Lily)
====================

Business operations AI for InalhaSoft indie game studio.

Status: Production

Overview
--------
Lily is a specialized AI agent handling business operations for InalhaSoft,
including communications, content creation, market research, and stakeholder
management. Features 3 sub-agents for specialized tasks.

My Role: Agent Architect & Business Systems Designer

Architecture
------------
- Main Model: GLM-5 Cloud (business-optimized)
- Sub-Agents: Mistral 7B (fast, efficient)
- Platform: Hermes Agent with sub-agent delegation
- Interface: Telegram Bot

Sub-Agent Specialization
------------------------
- Research: Market research, competitor analysis
- Social: Social media content, posts
- Analytics: Metrics, KPIs, reports

Capabilities
------------
- Business communications (professional emails, announcements)
- Planning (project timelines, milestone tracking)
- Content creation (social media posts, marketing copy)
- Market research (competitor analysis, industry trends)
- Analytics (KPI tracking, performance reports)
